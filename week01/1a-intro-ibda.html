Interpretation of Probability

Bayesian: a probability measures the relative plausibility of an event.

Frequentist: a probability measures the long-run relative frequency of a repeatable event. In fact, ‚Äúfrequentists‚Äù are so named because of their interpretation of probability as a long-run relative frequency.
